# Starlight Chronicle — Hero Image (State of the Palace)

## CONCEPT
A still architectural courtyard at the moment dawn breaks. The space holds eight tall, slender stone archways arranged in a wide gentle arc — composed plurality, each distinct, all in formation. The image is a witness, not a claim: a quiet sovereign space where work has been built and now rests.

## ART DIRECTION
Photographic, cinematic, calm. Restraint over flourish. Mediterranean limestone architecture rendered with soft realism. No people, no signage, no text, no symbols, no glow effects, no magical light, no holograms, no glyphs, no ornamentation. The architecture itself is the subject.

## SCENE
Smooth limestone flagstone courtyard, viewed from a low contemplative angle. Eight slender stone archways arc gently across the middle distance, evenly spaced, varying subtly in height. Beyond them, the horizon line meets a sky in transition. Long restful shadows fall across the stone toward the camera.

## COMPOSITION
Wide cinematic 16:9. Eyeline low, ~1m from the ground. Central archway slightly off-axis to the right; rule-of-thirds horizon at the lower third. Generous negative space in the upper two-thirds where sky breathes. Foreground stone leads the eye into the arc.

## LIGHTING
First warm light of dawn breaking from behind the central arch. Long, soft shadows. Ambient skylight fills the rest. No artificial light source. Contrast moderate — neither dramatic chiaroscuro nor flat. The hour is the moment before the day begins.

## PALETTE
Cream and warm sand limestone. Deep navy upper sky transitioning through indigo and dusty rose to warm coral and pale gold at the horizon. Shadow tones in cool taupe. No saturated primaries. Total palette: ~6 hues, all desaturated and natural.

## MOOD
Sovereign stillness. Composed without emptiness. The feeling of a palace courtyard the moment before its inhabitants begin the day — readiness held in repose. Witness, not announcement.

## STYLE REFERENCES
Andreas Gursky stillness; Luigi Ghirri palette restraint; the architectural quietude of Tadao Ando's exterior light studies. NOT: fantasy concept art, AI-generated dreamscapes, neon, glowing portals, light beams from heaven, lens flare, particle effects, fog overload.

## TYPOGRAPHY
None. No letterforms anywhere in the image.
