# Skill Selection Strategy for Open-Source Release

## Free Tier Skills (5 Skills - Marketing & Community Building)

### Selection Criteria:
1. **High value** - Demonstrates quality of the library
2. **Broad appeal** - Useful to many developers/creators
3. **Not unique differentiators** - Don't give away competitive advantages
4. **Showcases depth** - Shows the comprehensive nature of your work
5. **Gateway skills** - Creates desire for premium skills

### Selected Free Skills:

#### 1. Greek Philosopher ✅
**Why Free:**
- Unique and engaging (attracts attention)
- Not directly monetizable elsewhere
- Shows quality and depth
- Creates viral potential (people will share philosophical AI interactions)
- Gateway to Spartan Warrior (premium)

**Marketing Value**: HIGH - This will get shared on social media

#### 2. Spartan Warrior ✅
**Why Free:**
- Pairs with Greek Philosopher (complementary)
- Motivational content is highly shareable
- Shows your poetic/creative skill design
- Not enterprise-critical (won't cannibalize enterprise sales)

**Marketing Value**: HIGH - Motivational content spreads

#### 3. MCP Architecture Expert ✅
**Why Free:**
- Developer-focused (builds developer community)
- Foundational knowledge (pairs with paid skills)
- Shows technical expertise
- MCP is open protocol (aligns with open-source ethos)
- Gateway to Claude SDK, OpenAI AgentKit, Oracle ADK (all premium)

**Marketing Value**: VERY HIGH - Developers will star and share

#### 4. UI/UX Design Expert ✅
**Why Free:**
- Extremely valuable and comprehensive
- WCAG 2.2 compliance info is publicly available (not proprietary)
- Shows the depth of your research and synthesis
- Large potential audience (all designers)
- Gateway to Next.js/React Expert, Product Management (both premium)

**Marketing Value**: VERY HIGH - Designers desperately need WCAG 2.2 guidance

#### 5. Social Media Strategy ✅
**Why Free (when created):**
- Marketing-focused (attracts content creators)
- Broad appeal across industries
- Creates desire for FrankX-specific implementation (project skill)
- Shows practical business value
- Gateway to Video Production, Suno AI (both premium)

**Marketing Value**: HIGH - Content creators are influencers
